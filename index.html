Lane Detection and Traffic Sign Detection using Deep Learning and Computer Vision for Autonomous Driving Research Using CARLA Simulator

2023 International Journal on Recent and Innovation Trends in Computing and Communication (IJRITCC) Volume: 11 Issue: 10

Leveraging the power of Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN), specifically SegNet for semantic segmentation and You Only Look Once (YOLO) algorithm for object detection, the proposed Advanced Driver Assistance System (ADAS) demonstrates remarkable results. By simulating diverse urban driving scenarios using the CARLA simulator, the models showcase robust performance in dynamic and adverse environments. For lane detection, the SegNet-based model achieves an overall accuracy of 94.80%. In traffic sign detection, the model achieves an accuracy of 95.56%. These exceptional outcomes validate the efficacy of our models in enhancing safety and efficiency for autonomous vehicles.
